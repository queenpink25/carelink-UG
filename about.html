About CareLink

CareLink UG provides trusted, loving caregivers for all ages. We match caregivers with families based on the age and needs of your loved ones.

Who We Are

CareLink UG is a dedicated team of professionals committed to connecting families with compassionate and reliable caregivers. Our promise is to ensure that every individual, regardless of age, receives the care and attention they deserve in the comfort of their own home.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide compassionate, high-quality care that enhances the lives of our clients and their families.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion: We believe in treating every client and caregiver with kindness and empathy.
  • Integrity: We maintain honesty and transparency in all our dealings.
  • Respect: We treat everyone with dignity and consideration.

Our Team

Meet the dedicated professionals behind CareLink UG. Our team is passionate about providing exceptional care and support to families in need.

Services by Age Group:

Guidance, companionship, respectful supervision.

Adult (18–59 years)

Senior (60+ years)

Companionship, mobility assistance, medication reminders, emotional support.
