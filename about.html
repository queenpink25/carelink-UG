About CareLink

CareLink UG provides trusted, loving caregivers for all ages. We match caregivers with families based on the age and needs of your loved ones.

Services by Age Group:

Infant (0–2 years)

Feeding, nap routines, diaper changes, safe play.

Toddler (3–5 years)

Play-based learning, supervision, nurturing activities.

Child (6–12 years)

Homework help, creative activities, safe supervision.

Teen (13–17 years)

Guidance, companionship, respectful supervision.

Adult (18–59 years)

Daily tasks, companionship, light household support.

Senior (60+ years)

Companionship, mobility assistance, medication reminders, emotional support.
